About us

Welcome to CharityXI Match, where the spirit of sportsmanship meets the power of giving! In this extraordinary event, we bring together renowned football stars from the Premier League and around the globe for a game that goes beyond competition – a game for a cause.

Uniting for a Purpose

CharityXI Match is not just about showcasing the incredible skills of these football giants; it's a collective effort to make a positive impact on the world. In collaboration with esteemed partners such as UNICEF and other NGOs, this event aims to contribute to meaningful causes that resonate globally.

Global Icons, Global Impact

Imagine witnessing your favorite football heroes, the icons of the Premier League and international football, coming together on one pitch. The likes of legends from various teams and countries will join forces, demonstrating the unifying power of sport in support of humanitarian initiatives.

Join Us in Making a Difference

We invite you to be a part of this incredible journey – a celebration of football, compassion, and the unwavering belief that together, we can create positive change. By supporting CharityXI Match, you are not just enjoying a thrilling football spectacle; you are actively participating in a movement to build a better, more inclusive world.

Mark your calendars and stay tuned for updates as CharityXI Match approaches. Let's kick off a game that transcends borders and scores for humanity!
